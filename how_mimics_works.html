The science

Why Cell Mimics
Replace Cells

Through the intersection of biochemistry, polymer chemistry and high-precision manufacturing, Slingshot has created synthetic cell mimics that behave similarly to real cells — the world's first on-demand, synthetic cell design platform to address the industry's reliance on biologically-derived cellular controls.

The result matches the optical, fluorescence and biochemical features of biological cells without sacrificing performance or consistency. Shelf stable, on-demand, at scale.

Application 01

Assay Development

Engineer custom controls for biomarker assays, immune monitoring, and novel endpoints. Standardize assays from translational research through pivotal trials, reduce operator variability, and enable clean transfers between research and GLP/GCP environments.

Application 02

Laboratory Diagnostics

Match biomarker expression to specific disease states, including rare and emerging indications where commercial reference material doesn't exist. Tune antigen density to model disease severity — not just presence.

Application 03

Adoptive Cell Therapy

Validate potency, identity, and purity endpoints with custom controls for CAR-T enumeration, transgene expression, and viral vector characterization. Build the controls cell therapy manufacturing needs, even when no commercial reference exists.

Application 04

Drug Development

Cut variability, cost, and timeline risk across the development pipeline. Stable, reproducible reagents reduce complex workflows historically needed to normalize data for clinical trials — so multi-site studies stay consistent from Phase 1 to approval.

Application 05

Cell Analysis Instrument Performance

Calibration, compensation, and unmixing controls engineered for cross-platform consistency. Tech errors become immediately visible as peaks stand out against a stable baseline — enabling targeted training and site integration QC.
