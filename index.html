The science

Biological controls vary.
TruCytes® don't.

Traditional biomarker controls introduce inherent variability — donor variation, cold-chain degradation, ethical procurement barriers, and batch-to-batch inconsistency that inflates your error bars and slows your research.

TruCytes® are fully synthetic cell mimics engineered to exact specification. Every lot is manufactured to match your assay, verified by multi-parameter flow cytometry, and shipped with a Certificate of Analysis.
