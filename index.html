Inside every
TruCyte® particle

Three independently tunable engineering dimensions — each calibrated to your exact assay requirements. Click a property to explore it in the cell diagram.

Select a property to explore →
Calibrated Cell Size & Rigidity Cell Mechanics

Accurate calibration of cell size and rigidity for cell mechanics assays. TruCytes® are manufactured to precise diameter specifications and tunable elastic modulus — from highly deformable red-blood-cell analogues to stiff apoptotic-body mimics.

Relevant for: deformability cytometry · RT-DC · microfluidic squeeze assays · size calibration beads · FSC scatter gating

Tunable Granularity SSC Control

Tunable granularity to match cell types and states for accurate characterization. Internal complexity is independently engineered to produce precise side-scatter (SSC) signals — from agranulocyte-low to granulocyte-high — enabling reliable gating across differential white blood cell panels.

Relevant for: flow cytometry gating · leukocyte differential · hematology analyzer calibration · spectral unmixing reference populations

Embedded DNA Molecular Controls

Embedded DNA for viability assessments, NGS, qPCR validations, and single-cell sequencing applications. Reference or custom nucleic acid sequences are encapsulated at defined copy number inside the particle core — sequence-verified before every shipment.

Relevant for: NGS spike-in normalization · ddPCR standard curves · FISH controls · scRNA-seq QC · viability discrimination
